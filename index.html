How Travel Agency Work

When a client contacts a travel agency, they typically begin with a consultation to discuss their travel preferences, budget, destination, and any specific requirements or preferences they may have..Based on the information gathered during the consultation, the travel agent will research various travel options, including flights, accommodations, transportation, and any other services the client may need. They will then create a customized itinerary tailored to the client's preferences and budget. Once the client approves the proposed itinerary, the travel agent will proceed to book the necessary travel arrangements. This may include booking flights, hotels, rental cars,and any other services required for the trip.
